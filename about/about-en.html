Psychologist and IT Analyst

My name is Matias. Originally from Argentina, I now reside in Brazil. At 30 years old, I have a strong background in psychology and a passion for software development. During my career in psychology, I have worked in various areas, including education, work, forensic, and clinical. These experiences have allowed me to deeply understand people's needs and develop effective communication and teamwork skills.


In addition to my experience in psychology, I am an IT Analyst and have worked on personal projects related to software development. This second career has given me the opportunity to explore new ways of thinking and understand the technical mindset, which operates differently from social sciences.


I am motivated by my desire to learn and my interest in leveraging new technologies to benefit users and clients. I believe that my unique combination of skills in psychology and software development uniquely positions me to build strong relationships in work teams and understand clients' needs.


If you are interested in collaborating with me or learning more about my work, I would be happy to connect with you!
